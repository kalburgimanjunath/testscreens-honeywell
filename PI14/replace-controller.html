Please Note

Replacing an existing alarm control panel is a relatively straight forward task but it does require some forethought. Consider the following :- - Isolate the mains electricity supply to the panel - Label & Remove the connections and isolate the old controller - Connect New Controller. Ensure the MAC is not previously added in MAXPRO Cloud - While dis-associating the old hardware, the local data on the hardware will be erased completely
